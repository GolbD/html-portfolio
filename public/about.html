About Me

My philosophy is grounded in the concept that all human is caring. Thus with this back drop, I mold my knowledge, experience, devotion and commitment to ensure that my students are encouraged to establish and achieve their several goals. I am extremely passionate regarding the effective instruction and learning of students. I believe in leading by example. In the classroom, I therefore am very involved in providing incentives to learning. My students will be engaged in dialogue and discussion with the purpose of discovering deeper meaning and understanding of the subjects being studied, and the application to real life situations. My purpose in the classroom is to supply the tools that drive students to academic success, while recognizing that true success cannot be achieved without communication and collaboration.

I am very aware of the multiracial dynamics within the classroom and in society at large. I demonstrate and encourage respect, not only to the class as a community, but in all interactions. Value of self and honesty are principles I uphold and model to students, teaching them that ethical principles must drive their conduct and practice throughout all experiences in life. The diversity of student experiences dictate that a variety of learning methods be made available. Implementing appropriate methods, I will strive to awaken interest, creativity, devotion and curiosity that will propel each one to achievements beyond their immediate circumstances. I am consumed by these unknown, but eventual possibilities.

Life is made up of all the choices we make. Some choices are constructive while others are destructive. The important principle here is that we own those choices and learn from their impact. Students appreciate the philosophy I bring to the class. They learn to be empowered, assertive, proactive, independent as well as cooperative. They are encouraged to make learning fun. Students will recognize the importance of this philosophy, and own it as they study and interact successfully throughout each course. They will have the knowledge, the confidence, the skills, the wisdom and boldness to go where no one has gone before.
